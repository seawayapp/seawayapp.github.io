The Supposition Method

A P5 Singapore Math strategy — assume first, then adjust

Key Concept

Supposition means making an assumption to simplify the problem. We suppose all items are the same type, calculate the total under that assumption, then use the difference to find the real answer. No algebra needed!

5
Quick summary card
  1. Assume all items = smaller type → calculate supposed total
  2. Actual total − Supposed total = Extra amount
  3. Extra ÷ (bigger type − smaller type) = No. of bigger type
  4. Total items − bigger type = No. of smaller type
  5. Always verify by substituting back!
